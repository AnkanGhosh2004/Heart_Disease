About CardioSense

CardioSense is an intelligent web application designed to help users assess their risk of heart disease using advanced machine learning technology. Our mission is to empower individuals with accessible, science-backed health insights, enabling early awareness and proactive care.

Key Features

  • Easy-to-use Interface: Enter your health details in a simple, guided form.
  • Instant Prediction: Get immediate feedback on your heart disease risk based on your inputs.
  • Data Privacy: Your information is never stored or shared. All predictions are made in real-time and are confidential.
  • Educational: Learn about key heart health indicators and what they mean for you.

How It Works

CardioSense uses a machine learning model trained on real-world heart disease data. By analyzing your age, cholesterol, blood pressure, ECG results, and other factors, it provides a personalized risk assessment. This tool is intended for educational and awareness purposes and does not replace professional medical advice.

Why Heart Disease?

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide, but many risk factors are manageable with early detection and lifestyle changes. CardioSense aims to make heart health information accessible to everyone, encouraging timely consultation with healthcare professionals.

Disclaimer: CardioSense is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for any health concerns.
